Strategy tiers

Conservative

Lower risk

Primarily PoS staking and blue‑chip LSTs. Goal: capital preservation with steady rewards.

  • Assets: LSTs (e.g., stETH), major PoS tokens
  • Target range (illustrative): 4–8% APY
  • Auto‑compounding, slashing insurance optional
Select Conservative →

Balanced

Medium risk

Blend of staking, restaking, and stable‑pair LPs with capped exposure to new protocols.

  • Assets: LST/LRT, stable LP vaults
  • Target range (illustrative): 8–15% APY
  • Drawdown guardrails and weekly rebalance
Select Balanced →

Growth

Higher risk

Opportunistic restaking, points campaigns, and programmatic LP strategies. Strict position sizing.

  • Assets: LRTs, rotating incentives
  • Target range (illustrative): 15–25%+ APY
  • Daily risk checks and alerting

Frequently asked questions

Is this custodial? Who controls the assets?

SmartMine is non‑custodial. You authorize strategies from your own wallet, and you can withdraw or revoke at any time.

What chains are supported?

We aim to support major EVM chains at launch, with additional networks based on demand. Exact chain list may vary during beta.

What are the risks?

Smart contracts, slashing, depeg events, market volatility, and oracle failures are among the key risks. We use guardrails and monitoring, but risks cannot be eliminated.

Do I need to run hardware?

No dedicated hardware is required for most strategies. If you choose to operate a node or validator, we provide guides and templates.

Fees?

During beta we target no management fee and a performance‑based fee on realized rewards, shown clearly before confirmation. Fees are subject to change and will be disclosed.

Is this available in my region?

Availability depends on local laws and your residency. You are responsible for complying with applicable regulations in your jurisdiction.
