Strategy tiers

Conservative

Lower risk

Primarily PoS staking and blue-chip LSTs. Goal: capital preservation with steady rewards.

  • Assets: LSTs (e.g., stETH), major PoS tokens
  • Target range (illustrative): 4–8% APY
  • Auto-compounding, slashing insurance optional

Balanced

Medium risk

Blend of staking, restaking, and stable-pair LPs with capped exposure to new protocols.

  • Assets: LST/LRT, stable LP vaults
  • Target range (illustrative): 8–15% APY
  • Drawdown guardrails and weekly rebalance

Growth

Higher risk

Opportunistic restaking, points campaigns, and programmatic LP strategies. Strict position sizing.

  • Assets: LRTs, rotating incentives
  • Target range (illustrative): 15–25%+ APY
  • Daily risk checks and alerting
