Frequently asked questions

Is this custodial? Who controls the assets?

We are non-custodial. You approve strategies from your own wallet, and you can withdraw or revoke at any time.

What chains are supported?

Major EVM chains at launch, with more based on demand. Exact list may vary during beta.

What are the risks?

Smart contracts, slashing, de-peg events, market volatility, and oracle failures. We use guardrails and monitoring, but risks remain.

Do I need to run hardware?

Not for most strategies. If you want to run a node/validator, we provide guides and templates.

Fees?

During beta we target no management fee and a performance-based fee on realized rewards. Fees may change and are disclosed clearly before confirmation.

Is this available in my region?

Availability depends on your local laws. You are responsible for complying with regulations in your jurisdiction.
